Cassell Coliseum

The beautiful archways next to Cassell Coliseum tower above the athletic region of the Virginia Tech campus. These archways are located next to Lane Stadium, surrounded by colorful flowers.

Duck Pond Bench

The Duck Pond is one of Virginia Tech’s most popular spots on campus. The bench faces the scenic water where visitors can take in the beautiful views.

Swimming Duck

The swimming duck is only one example of all the animals that live at the Duck Pond. Visitors can see all of the wildlife living in the pond and relax in a calming environment.

Two Tree Hammocks

Two students chose to enjoy the beautiful Blacksburg weather in tree hammocks. The hammocks are surrounding by falling leaves as the Fall season is starting.

White House

The White House is located in the academic region of the Virginia Tech campus. The old architecture makes this building unique. The old white shutters give the house a rustic feel.

Lane Stadium

Lane Stadium is the essence of Virginia Tech culture. The massive stadium holds the home games for the Virginia Tech Hokies. In the South Endzone, past Virginia Tech players names fly high to remember their excellence and dedication.

The Stream

The stream located near the entrance of the Duck Pond is a hidden treasure. The calming environment surrounding the creek is another example of Virginia Tech’s beautiful campus. The sunlight floods through the rich trees to create this radiant sunshine.

The Drillfield

The Drillfield is the center of the Virginia Tech campus. Students are often seen rushing to class, playing sports, or enjoying a sunny day. The luscious green field is coated in trees shedding leaves in preparation for the Fall season.

Virginia Tech vs. Notre Dame

The energy in Lane Stadium was electric as the Hokies faced off against undefeated Notre Dame. Virginia Tech cheered on the Hokies even though we lost the long-awaited game. Metallica kicked off the game by introducing Enter Sandman, and the stadium jumped with 65,632 of our fellow Hokies!

Three Little Birds

Two ducks are joined by a white Pekin as they swim in the Duck Pond. The birds swam in unison as the light peered through the trees. Plenty of birds swam in the pond while others stayed on land in search of bread from visitors.
